🎯 詞性題核心策略
策略①先看空格前後的句子結構，判斷空格需要什麼詞性（名詞 / 形容詞 / 副詞 / 動詞）
策略②認識字尾（suffix）是解題關鍵：同一字根可衍生出名詞、動詞、形容詞、副詞
策略③注意「乍看像形容詞但其實是名詞」的字尾：-ive（initiative, alternative）、-al（potential, professional）
策略④動名詞 -ing 可作名詞使用（subject / object of verb）
⚠️ 特別注意：乍看像形容詞的名詞
-ive 字尾 → 可以是名詞！
initiative（主動；倡議）
alternative（替代方案）
objective（目標）
-al 字尾 → 可以是名詞！
professional（專業人士）
potential（潛力）
manual（手冊）
例：Since you seem stressed out, you should consult with a professional.
→ professional 在此是名詞「專業人士」，不是形容詞

📐 名詞子句假設語氣（Subjunctive Mood）

當主要子句中出現特定的動詞、形容詞、或名詞時，其後的 that 子句需使用原形動詞（動詞原形），無論主詞是誰。這是多益 Part 5 的高頻考點。

觸發動詞（+ that + S + V原形）
recommend, request, propose, suggest, demand, insist, advise
Ms. Lee suggested that I speak with the sales manager first thing tomorrow morning.
（speak 用原形，不用 speaks 或 spoke）
觸發形容詞（It is ___ that + S + V原形）
important, necessary, essential, imperative, vital, crucial
It is imperative that Jeff contact his client immediately.
（contact 用原形，不用 contacts 或 contacted）
觸發名詞（the ___ that + S + V原形）
recommendation, request, proposal, suggestion, demand, advice
The board approved the proposal that the plant in Beijing be reopened.
（be 用原形，不用 is 或 was）
⚠️ 考試重點： 被動語態時用 be + p.p.（不是 is/was/were）
例：It is recommended that a business plan be developed. ✅（不是 is developed / was developed）

📄 文章：Artist Walks to Find Creativity
Artist and digital content manager Stacy Aham attributes her time-management skills and creative success to a simple habit—a daily walk. Ms. Aham [8] it a point to walk every day, at least for one mile, around her neighborhood.

"It helps me prepare for the day ahead," said Ms. Aham. "About six months ago, I canceled my gym memberships and exercise classes. I basically decided to leave my car on the driveway most of the time, and now I just walk [9]. I'm the healthiest I've ever been. Wandering around free of purpose also helps me discover artistic ideas," she says. "The natural surroundings inspire me. [10]."

Ms. Aham [11] feels more confidence in her ideas and is better able to focus on her goals.
